Margin Recovery

Resolving revenue leakage & margin erosion in high-scale PMPs

Situation

A strategic agency partner was scaling cross-channel spend on TTD and DV360, but gross margins on custom PMPs kept sliding: unoptimized platform costs, fluctuating data fees, mismatched floor configurations.

Action
  • Ran an exhaustive deal-level P&L breakdown of tech fees, inventory rebates and cost allocations.
  • Queried programmatic transaction logs with SQL, uncovering buyers bypassing private auction tiers through overlapping inclusion logic in GAM.
  • Implemented a dynamic floor pricing matrix that adjusts bids in real time on historical context, no manual intervention.
Result

Plugged a 4.5% revenue leakage gap, recovered gross margins to corporate compliance, and standardized a PMP commercial playbook now used globally.

01 · The Problem

Fragmentation at scale

Large retail media networks fragment fast when massive budgets scale across multi-screen blocks. Without a bidirectional read on the auction, accounts suffer audience duplication, mismatched SSP floor prices, and erratic spend speeds that dilute ROAS.

02 · Inventory Engineering

Both sides of the auction, linked

Linked buy-side DSP auction parameters directly with supply-side floor constraints inside GAM, securing premium brand-safe supply lines before they hit open bidding pools.

03 · Funnel Layering

Multi-screen, top to bottom

Wide-reach CTV, OTT, and OLV for upper-funnel awareness, with lower-funnel display retargeting loops picking up intent signals cleanly.

04 · Adaptive Control

70–100 campaigns, automated bids

Balanced 70–100 concurrent campaigns across open web and closed social, with automated bid adjusters in The Trade Desk Kokai reacting to supply-side viewability signals.

05 · The Result

Absolute budget governance

Spend held tightly on a 98% pacing target while inventory value was maximized across every transaction tier: PMP, PG, and open exchange.
