Executive Narrative
01 · Signal

An anomaly no dashboard flagged

Creatives began failing approval at volume during a high-spend window. Surface metrics read “disapproved” — a dead end that would normally trigger a creative rework loop and days of lost pacing.

02 · Diagnosis

Rejecting the obvious answer

Rather than rebuild assets, I isolated the pattern: the failures were uniform, not creative-specific. That pointed away from the creative and toward the delivery path itself.

03 · Root Cause

One line, blocking an entire crawler

The ads verification crawler was disallowed in robots.txt while the standard index crawler remained open — so the platform could never fetch the landing page to verify it. Not a media problem. An infrastructure problem.

04 · Resolution

Confirmed, escalated, cleared

I documented the exact mechanism and the fix, escalated with evidence, and had it externally confirmed by the platform in writing — clearing the rejections before client-visible impact.

05 · Systemized

Designed out for good

Crawlability is now a standing pre-launch QA check-gate. A one-time forensic win became a permanent control — the essence of FMEA in media.
