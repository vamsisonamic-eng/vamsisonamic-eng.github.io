Executive Narrative
01 · Signal

An anomaly no dashboard flagged

Creatives started failing approval at volume during a high-spend window. The surface metric just said "disapproved." Left alone, that reads as a creative problem and burns days on a rework loop.

02 · Diagnosis

Rejecting the obvious answer

Instead of rebuilding assets, I looked at the pattern. The failures were uniform, not creative-specific. That pointed away from the creative and toward the delivery path.

03 · Root Cause

One line, blocking an entire crawler

The ads verification crawler was disallowed in robots.txt while the standard index crawler stayed open. The platform could never fetch the landing page to verify it. This was never a media problem. It was an infrastructure one.

04 · Resolution

Confirmed, escalated, cleared

I wrote up the exact mechanism and the fix, escalated with evidence, and had it confirmed by the platform in writing. The rejections cleared before any client noticed.

05 · Systemized

Designed out for good

Crawlability is now a standing pre-launch QA check-gate. A one-time forensic win became a permanent control. That is what FMEA looks like in media.
